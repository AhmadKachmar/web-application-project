About Us

Making Math Fun with Scratch!

At Scratch-Math, we are passionate about making math enjoyable and accessible for kids. We believe that learning math does not have to be boring—it can be creative, interactive, and fun! That's why we use Scratch, a block-based programming language, to teach essential math concepts in an engaging way.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help students:

  • Develop a strong foundation in mathematics
  • Learn to think logically and solve problems creatively
  • Gain hands-on experience with coding and technology

By combining math and coding, we prepare young learners for a future where computational thinking is essential!

Who Is This For?

This website is designed for:

  • Students who want to learn math through interactive activities
  • Teachers looking for innovative ways to teach math concepts
  • Parents who want to support their children's learning with fun, engaging tools

Project Inspiration

The idea for Scratch-Math came from a simple question: How can we make math more engaging for students? Many learners struggle with traditional math lessons, finding them dull or difficult to understand. At the same time, coding is becoming an essential skill for the future.

We realized that Scratch, a fun and visual programming language, could bridge the gap between math and interactive learning. By turning numbers into animations, equations into interactive games, and problem-solving into hands-on coding challenges, we created a way for students to learn math by doing—not just by memorizing.

This project was insd by educators, students, and the growing need for creative, technology-driven learning experiences. Our goal is to help kids see the magic in math and build skills that will benefit them in the digital age!
